Musings of an Indian

On farming, living well, and the long view.

About

Paa Sankar writes about the ideas and practices that occupy his thinking: organic farming and the science of healthy soil, independent living that carries its own weight without extracting from others, leveraging AI as a tool for genuinely meaningful outcomes, educating the next generation to think clearly and act wisely, and studying history — not as a museum of the past but as a working document for today's problems.

These are not separate interests. They are the same inquiry approached from different angles.
